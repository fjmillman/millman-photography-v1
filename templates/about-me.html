Fred Millman is a Yorkshire-born student of Computer Science based in the UNESCO world heritage city of Bath in the United Kingdom.

He began his path into photography by picking up a DSLR and joining up with the University of Bath's Photography Society in his first year.

Over the next few years, he would begin to explore a new way of seeing the world around him and take advantage of the opportunities that the Photography Society had to offer.

Fred's main interests in photography lie firmly in Landscapes, although he has dabbled in Portraits, and Architecture.
